## 9. Recommendations for MECANIX

### MUST ADD (Pre-Launch or Phase 1.5)

1. **Online booking via Customer App** — Every competitor has online scheduling. Add appointment booking to the Customer App (customers pick date/time, select service type). Already partially designed — just needs the booking flow.

2. **DVI with Video** — Add short video capture (30-60 seconds) to the existing DVI feature. Photos alone are table-stakes; video is the new standard. Tekmetric, Shop-Ware, AutoLeap all support it.

3. **Workshop branding on Customer App** — GaragePlug offers a white-label customer app per workshop. MECANIX should show the workshop's logo, colors, and name prominently in the Customer App when a customer is viewing their job.

### SHOULD ADD (Phase 2)

4. **AI WhatsApp Auto-Responder** — The AO/MZ equivalent of AutoLeap's AI Receptionist. Auto-respond to common WhatsApp inquiries (status checks, appointment requests, quote questions). This is a massive differentiator for markets where WhatsApp is primary.

5. **Fleet/Corporate Account Management** — Shop-Ware, AutoFluent, and GarageBox all support fleet accounts with multi-contact billing, PO requirements, and fleet statements. Relevant for workshops serving companies and government vehicles.

6. **Google Reviews / Rating Integration** — Where applicable (urban markets), auto-prompt customers to leave Google reviews after service. Tekmetric, AutoLeap, GarageBox all have this.

7. **Accounting Software Integration** — QuickBooks (global), Primavera (Angola), or at minimum CSV export that accounting software can import. GarageBox, Workshop Software, Evolv all integrate with accounting.

8. **CRM Module** (leads, tasks, follow-ups) — GarageBox and Tekmetric have full CRMs. At minimum, track leads (walk-in inquiries, phone calls) and follow up on abandoned quotes.

9. **Document Renewal Reminders** — Remind customers when vehicle license, insurance, or inspection certificate expires. Highly relevant in AO/MZ. GarageBox has this.

10. **Barcode/QR Scanning for Parts** — GarageBox, AutoFluent, GaragePlug all support barcode scanning for inventory management. Use the phone camera for quick stock lookups.

11. **AMC (Maintenance Contract) Packages** — Allow workshops to sell annual maintenance packages (e.g., 4 oil changes + 2 inspections for fixed annual price). GarageBox has this. Drives predictable revenue.

12. **Marketing Campaigns via WhatsApp** — Automated seasonal campaigns (e.g., "AC check before summer"), promotions, and re-engagement messages to lapsed customers. Multiple competitors have email campaigns — adapt for WhatsApp.

### CONSIDER (Phase 3)

13. **AI Repair Assistant Chatbot** — Like Evolv's EvoAI, help technicians diagnose issues by describing symptoms. High value in markets with less experienced mechanics.

14. **AI Service Recommendations** — Based on vehicle history and mileage, auto-suggest services when a job card is created. Like GaragePlug's GPSense but simpler.

15. **Petty Cash Management** — Track daily petty cash transactions within the workshop. GarageBox has this. Very relevant for cash-heavy AO/MZ operations.

16. **Gate Pass Management** — Track vehicle entry/exit from workshop premises. GarageBox has this. Relevant for security in AO markets.

17. **Loyalty/Membership Program** — Points system or tiered membership (free oil change after 5 services). Drives retention. GarageBox has basic version.

18. **Customer Complaint Tracking** — Formal complaint registration and resolution tracking with SLA. GarageBox has this.

19. **AI Parts Pricing Optimization** — Like Shop-Ware's AI Parts Matrix, suggest optimal markup based on sales patterns and competition.

20. **Vehicle Health Reports** — Periodic automated reports sent to customers showing vehicle condition and upcoming maintenance. GaragePlug does this.
