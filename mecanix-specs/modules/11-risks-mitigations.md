# 11. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Workshop adoption resistance | High | High | Simple onboarding; free setup for first 20 customers; champion program |
| Connectivity issues causing data loss | High (AO/MZ) | High | Offline-first architecture; background sync; conflict resolution |
| Insurance company reluctance to adopt | Medium | High | Start with 1–2 insurer partnerships; prove ROI with pilot data; offer free onboarding |
| NF-e complexity delays Brazil entry | High | Medium | Treat Brazil as separate variant; hire Brazilian fiscal developer |
| WhatsApp Business API approval delays | Medium | Medium | Pre-apply during development; SMS fallback |
| Customer app low adoption | Medium | Medium | Incentivise via WhatsApp deep links; workshop staff promote at check-in |
| Time logging resistance from technicians | Medium | Medium | Gamification; make it dead simple (2 taps); show personal productivity stats |
| Insurance fraud via manipulated photos | Medium | High | GPS/timestamp validation; anomaly detection; audit trails |
| Price sensitivity in AO/MZ | Medium | Medium | Starter tier at $49; annual billing discount; local currency billing |
| Competing product launches | Low | Medium | Move fast; first-mover advantage; lock in case studies early |
