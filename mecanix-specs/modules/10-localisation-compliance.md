## 10.3 Payment Methods by Market

| Market | Cash | Bank Transfer | Card | Mobile Money | Notes |
|--------|------|---------------|------|-------------|-------|
| Angola | Yes | Yes | Yes | Multicaixa Express | USD payments common in corporate sector |
| Mozambique | Yes | Yes | Limited | M-Pesa (critical) | M-Pesa is dominant — Phase 1 priority |
| Brazil | Yes | PIX (critical) | Yes | N/A | PIX instant transfer is now the standard |

## 10.4 Insurance Regulatory Considerations

- **Angola:** ARSEG (insurance regulator) — no digital claims mandate yet; early mover advantage
- **Mozambique:** ISSM regulator — similar landscape; paper-based processes accepted
- **Brazil:** Susep-regulated; specific digital requirements for claims documentation — Phase 3 compliance

## 10.5 Data Protection

- GDPR-aligned data retention policies (configurable per market)
- Angola: Lei de Proteccao de Dados Pessoais (Law 22/11) — basic personal data protection
- Mozambique: No comprehensive data protection law yet — follow GDPR as baseline
- Brazil: LGPD (Lei Geral de Protecao de Dados) — full compliance required for Phase 3
